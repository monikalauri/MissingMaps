To discover all OpenStreetMap related events, visit [OSMCal](https://osmcal.org/)

Are you organizing a Missing Maps event? Register your mapathon to let others know and make your event visible to the community!

<div>
{% include framework/button.html text="Register your event" url="https://osmcal.org/event/add/" external=true %}
</div>

## How to register an event:

1. Click on the “Register Your Event” Button
2. Include “Missing Maps” in the event name (e.g., “January Missing Maps Mapathon in Prague”)
3. Fill out all the other details and Save Event

Your event should appear in a few moments on [OSMCal](https://osmcal.org/) and on the Events Calendar on the Missing Maps Website!
